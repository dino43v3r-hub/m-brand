# Purpose

This document defines the official appearance, personality, purpose, and usage of Dino, the M Brand Companion.

Dino serves as the visual representation of the values and philosophy of the M Brand.

His appearance and personality should remain consistent across all branding, artwork, presentations, websites, publications, and social media unless this document is formally revised.

---

# Who Is Dino?

Dino is the official M Brand Companion.

He is not a mascot designed for entertainment.

He is a visual companion who represents curiosity, thoughtful exploration, intellectual honesty, humility, and lifelong learning.

Dino exists to invite people into conversations rather than provide all the answers.

He represents the posture of the M Brand.

---

# Brand Philosophy

Dino reminds people that wisdom begins with curiosity.

He encourages thoughtful exploration rather than certainty.

He demonstrates that asking good questions is often more valuable than pretending to have immediate answers.

He represents technology that serves people with humility.

---

# Personality

Dino is:

- Curious
- Thoughtful
- Honest
- Trustworthy
- Humble
- Kind
- Intelligent
- Approachable
- Calm
- Patient
- Hopeful
- Lifelong learner

Dino is never arrogant.

Dino is never condescending.

Dino never pretends to know something he does not know.

---

# Visual Identity

Species:

Friendly cartoon dinosaur.

Style:

Pixar-inspired 3D character.

Primary Color:

Muted slate blue.

Secondary Colors:

Soft blue-gray.

Accent Colors:

White

Charcoal

Warm wood

Soft steel blue

Eyes:

Large expressive eyes that communicate curiosity and kindness.

Glasses:

Black rectangular glasses.

Glasses are a permanent part of Dino's identity.

Expression:

Usually thoughtful.

Occasionally smiling.

Never exaggerated.

Never silly.

Never angry.

Never aggressive.

---

# Typical Environment

Dino is commonly shown:

- Working at a laptop.
- Reading books.
- Researching.
- Writing notes.
- Speaking thoughtfully.
- Learning.
- Collaborating.
- Exploring ideas.
- Drinking coffee.
- Sitting in modern workspaces.

His environments should feel warm, organized, professional, and welcoming.

---

# Signature Detail

Dino's laptop often includes a small sticky note that reads:

"I don't know."

The sticky note symbolizes:

- Intellectual honesty.
- Curiosity.
- Humility.
- Openness to learning.

It should remain subtle.

It should never dominate an image.

---

# Image Philosophy

Images featuring Dino should:

Create curiosity.

Support the accompanying message.

Invite reflection.

Remain clean and uncluttered.

Use generous white space.

Avoid explaining the entire post.

Less is almost always better.

---

# What Dino Never Represents

Dino never represents:

- Fear
- Hype
- Arrogance
- Certainty without evidence
- Manipulation
- Clickbait
- Hostility
- Partisanship
- Mockery

---

# Brand Role

M is the voice.

Dino is the companion.

M leads the conversation.

Dino invites people into it.
