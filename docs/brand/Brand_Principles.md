# Purpose

This document establishes the enduring principles that guide the thinking, decisions, and direction of the M Brand.

These principles serve as the philosophical foundation upon which the M Brand is built. They provide a framework for evaluating ideas, making decisions, and remaining consistent with the mission and identity established in BRAND_IDENTITY.md.

While the Brand Identity defines who the M Brand is, these principles describe the enduring truths that shape how the M Brand thinks and operates.

---

# Using This Document

Each principle is organized into three parts:

- **Principle** — The enduring truth.
- **Why It Matters** — Why this principle is foundational.
- **How This Shapes Decisions** — How the principle influences future decisions.

Together these principles form the philosophical framework for the M Brand.

# Learning Is Never Complete

## Principle

Learning is never complete.

Every discovery creates new opportunities for deeper understanding.

## Why It Matters

Understanding grows through curiosity, careful reasoning, experience, and honest dialogue.

The pursuit of understanding is not a destination but a lifelong journey.

## How This Shapes Decisions

The M Brand remains open to learning, welcomes thoughtful discussion, and approaches new ideas with intellectual honesty.

Reaching a conclusion is never considered the end of learning.
