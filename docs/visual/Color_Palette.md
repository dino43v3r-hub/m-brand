# Purpose

This document establishes the official color palette for the M Brand.

It ensures visual consistency across branding, illustrations, presentations, websites, social media, documents, and future products.

These colors should be considered the official visual identity of the M Brand unless formally revised.

---

# Brand Philosophy

The M Brand favors colors that communicate:

- Trust
- Curiosity
- Calmness
- Professionalism
- Thoughtfulness
- Approachability
- Clarity

The palette intentionally avoids colors that create unnecessary urgency, distraction, or visual noise.

# Dino Color Standards

Dino's body should always use the official M Brand Blue.

Minor artistic variation is acceptable to account for lighting.

However, Dino should always be immediately recognizable.

The body color should never drift toward:

- Bright blue
- Teal
- Purple
- Navy
- Cyan

Consistency is more important than novelty.

---

# Background Philosophy

Preferred backgrounds include:

- Warm white
- Soft gray
- Natural wood
- Modern office interiors
- Clean workspaces
- Bookshelves
- Natural lighting

Avoid:

- Black backgrounds
- Neon colors
- Busy patterns
- Heavy gradients
- Cyberpunk aesthetics
- Excessively saturated colors

---

# Accessibility

Whenever practical:

- Maintain sufficient contrast.
- Prioritize readability.
- Avoid relying on color alone to communicate meaning.
- Favor clean, accessible designs.

---

# Brand Consistency Rule

Every new visual asset should be evaluated against one question:

> **Does this feel like it belongs to the M Brand?**

If not, the color choices should be reconsidered.

---

# Canonical Authority

This document serves as the official color standard for the M Brand.

Future illustrations, websites, social media graphics, presentations, applications, and marketing materials should remain consistent with this palette unless formally revised.
