title: "The Biggest Challenge Facing AI May Not Be Intelligence"
date: 2026-07-01
excerpt: |-
  One of the questions I've been returning to isn't how intelligent AI can become.

  It's whether we can trust it.

  The systems we rely on most may not be the ones that always have an answer. They may be the ones that explain their reasoning, acknowledge uncertainty, distinguish evidence from inference, and are willing to say, "I don't know."

  To me, intellectual honesty isn't a limitation of AI.

  It may become one of its greatest strengths.
