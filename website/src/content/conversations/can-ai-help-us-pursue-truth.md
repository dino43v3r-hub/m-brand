  One question has quietly shaped much of the work behind ConnectAloud.

  Can artificial intelligence help us pursue truth without replacing human reasoning?

  The more I've explored AI, the less interested I've become in building systems that think for people. Instead, I'm drawn to creating tools that help us reason with greater clarity, evaluate evidence more thoughtfully, and remain responsible for the conclusions we reach.

  Perhaps the future of AI isn't found in replacing human judgment, but in strengthening it.
