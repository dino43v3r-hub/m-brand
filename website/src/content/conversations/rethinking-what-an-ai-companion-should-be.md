title: "Rethinking What an AI Companion Should Be"
date: 2026-07-08
excerpt: |-
  While building Companion Core, I found myself rejecting almost every idea I had.

  I wasn't interested in creating another chatbot designed to keep people talking to AI.

  I wanted something different.

  An AI companion that helps people organize their thoughts, prepare for meaningful conversations, learn something new, and become more present with the people already in their lives.

  Perhaps the best AI companion isn't the one that replaces relationships.

  Perhaps it's the one that strengthens them.
whyItMatters: |-
  This conversation introduces the philosophy behind Companion Core. Rather than maximizing engagement with AI, it asks how technology can encourage stronger human relationships.
